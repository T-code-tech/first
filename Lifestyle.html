Let's talk about the life of the city

Shanghai is one of China's leading cities when it comes to career opportunities.
Money and money making are important aspects of the city culture. The use of cash is getting to be very rare.
The city is alive 24/7 and you will be able to order takeout and even get a haircut at any time of the day and night.
The rapid pace of life is a trait shared by most big cities, but Shanghai is notoriously fast. You will see people on streets starting from early as four oçlock in the morning and
the hustle doesn't quite down until late in the night. People in this city talk about money a lot and you should expect questions relating to your salary, rent, expenses etc. which are not considered rude
The stark contrast of posh high rises and small traditional lane houses is exaggerated even further by the striking contrast of
luxury high rises with tradional lane homes.
