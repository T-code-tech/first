EVENTS

Shanghai has hand-picked events for the people living there. Shining like a bright pearl in the oriental world with ever rising international position and influence, Shanghai is a place preferred by many for holding numerous varied events, including festivals, performances, competitions, exhibitions and convensions.
1. New year bell striking- This is a new year celebration whereby a bell is striked at 00:00 which takes place in the Longhua Temple. The bell is expected to strike 108 times.
2. The China International Import Expo- The expo is committed to trade liberalization and economic globalization. It takes place from the 5th to the 10th of November.
3. Shanghai World Travel- a travel exhibition co-organized by Shanghai Culture and Tourism Bureau that happens from April 23 to April 26. As one of the premier annual tourism procurement events in China, the World Travel Fair always insists on organizing high-quality tourism resources and tourism service companies at home and abroad, presenting rich and diverse tourism resources to domestic tourism buyers and many travel enthusiasts.
4. The Annual Ring Exxpo- Adhering to the excellent quality of the global environmental brand exhibition IFAT Germany for 50 years, the Annual Ring Expo in China will gather the world's top sewage treatment, water supply and drainage, solid waste treatment, Cutting-edge technologies and the latest solutions in the field of environmental pollution control. It takes place around April 23rd to 26.
